Biography

I am currently a graduate student at the School of Automation, Southeast University, P.R. China. He worked at the Key Laboratory of Measurement and Control of Complex Systems of Engineering, Ministry of Education, advised by Prof. Xianzhong Dai. Before that, he graduated with bachelor’s degree in Measurement & Control Technology and Instruments in July 2010 from Anhui University Of Technology, China. His research interest covers multiple robots cooperation, machine learning, computer vision and artificial intelligence.
