Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering at McMaster University, supervised by Prof. T. Kirubarajan. Before that, I worked as a research assistant with the Department of Computing at Hong Kong Polytechnic University during 2018.8~2019.7, supervised by Prof. Lei Zhang. I received the M.Eng. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Southeast University(SEU), Nanjing, China, in 2018, supervised by Prof. Xianzhong Dai ; the B.Eng. degree in Measurement & Control Technology and Instruments from Anhui University of Technology(AHUT), MaAnshan, China, in 2010. My research interest covers multiple robots cooperation, machine learning, computer vision and artificial intelligence.
